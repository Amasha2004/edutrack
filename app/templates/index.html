Everything You Need

A complete solution for managing students, teachers, and courses

🔐

Role-Based Access

Separate dashboards and permissions for Admins, Teachers, and Students with secure authentication.

📊

Grade Management

Teachers enter assignment, midterm and final scores. GPA is calculated automatically in real time.

📅

Attendance Tracking

Mark students present, absent or late per class. Students see their attendance percentage instantly.

📄

PDF Transcripts

Students can download a professional academic transcript with grades, GPA, and official signature.

📈

Analytics Dashboard

Visual charts showing grade distribution, attendance rates, enrollments per course and more.

🌙

Dark Mode

Full dark mode support with a single click toggle. Preference is saved across sessions.

Built for Everyone

Three powerful roles, each with their own tailored experience

👨‍💼

Admin

  • Manage all students & teachers
  • Create and assign courses
  • Manage departments
  • View analytics & charts
  • Full system control
👩‍🏫

Teacher

  • View assigned courses
  • Mark daily attendance
  • Enter & update grades
  • Track student progress
  • Manage class records
👨‍🎓

Student

  • View personal dashboard
  • Browse & enroll in courses
  • Check attendance records
  • View grades & GPA
  • Download PDF transcript
